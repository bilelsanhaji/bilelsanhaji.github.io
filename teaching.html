Teaching Philosophy

I emphasize the practical application of econometric and statistical methods to real-world economic and financial data. My courses combine theoretical foundations with hands-on computational exercises using software tools.

I encourage students to develop critical thinking skills and to understand not just how to apply methods, but when and why they are appropriate. Special attention is given to recent developments in the field, including machine learning applications in economics and finance.
